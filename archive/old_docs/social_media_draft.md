**🌿 The "Pork & Plants" Strategy**

This week is a bit unusual, Calgary. Most flyers are showing "Normal Prices" across the board as stores reset between seasons, but my scan found one massive outlier at Safeway and a battle for the best Pork roast between Co-op and No Frills.

**The Strategy: Two-Stop Shop.**
1.  **Calgary Co-op** for the main protein (Sunday Roast) and fresh produce.
2.  **Safeway** for a massive clearance deal on house plants (great gift idea) and specialty ground meats.

If you are strictly budget-focused, **No Frills** is your backup for cheap ribs and pantry fillers.
